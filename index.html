Tic Tac Toe with a Twist

Get three in a row to win, but watch out for disappearing moves!

The Twist:

  • When your opponent makes their 3rd move, your oldest move turns gray.
  • After you make your next move, your oldest gray move disappears.
  • This pattern continues: each move by your opponent grays your oldest move, and your next move makes it disappear.

Tip: Gray/blurry moves will disappear on next turn.

Strategy:

  • Plan ahead - early moves won't stay forever
  • Use disappearing moves to set up multiple winning chances

No ties in this version - the game continues until there's a winner!
