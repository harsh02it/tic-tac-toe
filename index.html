How to Play

Welcome to this classic Tic Tac Toe game with a fun twist! Get three in a row to win, just like always. But here's the catch:

  • The first move you make will disappear after your 7th move.
  • The second move you make will disappear after your 8th move, and so on.

Think strategically and plan your moves ahead to outsmart your opponent and be the last one standing!
